Getting up and running on Billiards.com takes only a few minutes. This guide walks you through creating your account, building your player profile, and taking your first steps into the platform — from finding a local venue to logging your first match result.

<Tip>
  The Billiards.com mobile app is available for iOS and Android. Once you create your account on the web, you can sign in on the app and access every feature from your phone — including live league standings, a nearby venue map, and quick match logging right after you finish a rack.
</Tip>

## Steps to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [billiards.com](https://www.billiards.com) and click **Sign up**. You can register with your email address or continue with Google or Facebook.

    Enter your email, choose a password, and verify your address using the confirmation link sent to your inbox. If you sign up with a social account, the verification step is handled automatically.

    <Note>
      Your email address is used for match notifications, league updates, and account security. Billiards.com does not share your email with third parties or venue owners.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    After verifying your account, you are taken directly to the profile setup screen. Complete the following fields to get the most accurate league matching and venue recommendations:

    - **Display name** — this is the name other players see on leaderboards and match records.
    - **Location** — enter your city or ZIP code so the platform can surface nearby venues and leagues.
    - **Skill level** — choose from Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, or Pro. This sets your starting handicap, which adjusts automatically as you log matches.
    - **Preferred games** — select the formats you play most: 8-ball, 9-ball, 10-ball, straight pool, or one-pocket.

    You can update any of these settings later from your account settings page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find a venue">
    From your dashboard, open the **Venue finder** tab or click **Find a venue** in the navigation bar. The finder shows a map and list view of pool halls, billiards clubs, and casual venues near your saved location.

    Use the filters to narrow results by:

    - Table type (bar box, 9-foot, snooker)
    - Open now
    - League host
    - User rating

    Click any listing to see full details including hours, pricing, table count, and upcoming events. Click **Save venue** to add it to your favorites for quick access later.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Join a league">
    Open the **Leagues** tab from your dashboard to browse leagues in your area. Leagues are organized by game format and skill bracket. The platform automatically highlights leagues that match your skill level and preferred games.

    Each league listing shows:

    - Format (8-ball, 9-ball, etc.)
    - Skill bracket
    - Session schedule (weekly, bi-weekly)
    - Current enrollment and available spots
    - Season start date

    Click **Join league** on any open league to submit your enrollment. Some leagues require approval from the league operator, in which case you receive a confirmation email once accepted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log your first match">
    After any session at a venue or in a casual game, log your results to start building your stats profile.

    From your dashboard, click **Log match**. Select the game format, enter your opponent's display name or player ID, choose who won each game in the set, and submit. The platform calculates your updated win rate and adjusts your handicap based on your opponent's rating relative to yours.

    <Info>
      You do not need to play in a registered league to log matches. Casual match logging is available for all account holders and still contributes to your personal stats.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
