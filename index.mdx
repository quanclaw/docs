## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [Billiards.com](https://www.billiards.com) with your email or social login. It's free to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your profile">
    Add your skill level, preferred games (8-ball, 9-ball, straight pool), and location so we can match you with the right venues and leagues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a venue or league">
    Use the venue finder to discover nearby pool halls, or browse open leagues in your area and sign up to compete.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start playing and tracking">
    Log your matches after each session to build your stats, improve your handicap rating, and climb the leaderboards.
  </Step>
</Steps>
