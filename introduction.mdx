## Key features

### Venue finder

The venue finder covers pool halls, billiards clubs, and casual bar venues across the country. Each listing includes table count, table type (7-foot bar boxes, 9-foot tournament tables, snooker tables), hours of operation, pricing, and user reviews. You can save favorite spots and get notified when a venue hosts an event.

### Leagues

Billiards.com supports organized leagues in 8-ball, 9-ball, 10-ball, straight pool, and one-pocket formats. Leagues are divided into skill brackets based on your handicap rating so every match is competitive. You can join an existing league in your area or start your own and invite players directly.

### Stats tracking

Every match you log contributes to your player profile. The stats engine tracks wins, losses, game type, opponent handicap, and key performance indicators like average balls per turn and break percentage. Over time, your profile builds a detailed picture of your strengths and where you can improve.

### Tournaments

Browse and enter local, regional, and online tournaments from your dashboard. The bracket tracker updates in real time so you can follow results as they come in, whether you're on-site competing or watching from home.

### Community

The community feed lets you share match results, post trick shots, and follow players you admire. Game-specific groups give you a focused space to discuss strategy, equipment, and upcoming events with players who share your preferred format.

## Who Billiards.com is for

Billiards.com is built for players at every level and for the people who run venues.

**Casual players** use the platform primarily to find places to play and discover local events. If you shoot a few racks a week without any formal competition, the venue finder and community features are your starting point.

**Competitive players** get the most value from league management, stats tracking, and the handicap system. If you play in leagues, enter tournaments, or want to measure your improvement against real data, the full feature set is designed around your needs.

**Venue owners** can claim and manage their listings, publish events, run in-house leagues, and engage directly with their regular customers through the platform.
