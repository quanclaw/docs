---
title: "Notification Preferences on Billiards.com"
description: "Control which emails, push notifications, and in-app alerts you receive from Billiards.com, and set them independently per channel."
---

Billiards.com sends notifications to keep you informed about your matches, league activity, messages, and account security. You can enable or disable each category independently, and for most notification types you can choose whether to receive them by email, push notification, or both. Taking a few minutes to configure your preferences means you only hear from us when it matters to you.

## Navigate to notification settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the account menu">
    Select your avatar or username in the top-right corner of any page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click **Settings** in the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Notifications">
    Choose **Notifications** from the left-hand settings navigation to open the Notifications Preferences page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notification categories

Each category below has an on/off toggle. When a category is enabled, you can further customize delivery by channel — email, push notification, or both — for each individual notification type within it.

### League & match

These notifications relate to your active league participation and scheduled matches.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Match reminders">
    Sent before a scheduled league match — by default, 24 hours and 2 hours before start time. You can adjust the reminder window in the timing options below the toggle.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Opponent confirmed">
    Notifies you when your opponent accepts or confirms the scheduled match details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Result submitted">
    Sent when a match result is submitted — either by you, your opponent, or a league administrator.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Standings updated">
    Alerts you when your division standings are recalculated after match results are posted.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Keep Match Reminders enabled so you never miss a scheduled league game. A single missed forfeit can affect your standing and your team's record.
</Tip>

### Tournaments

Stay informed at every stage of a tournament you've registered for.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Registration confirmed">
    Sent immediately after your tournament entry is accepted and payment (if required) is processed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Bracket published">
    Notifies you when the tournament bracket is finalized and your first match is assigned.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Match called">
    Sent when tournament staff announces that your match is ready to begin. Time-sensitive — push notification is recommended for this type.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Results posted">
    Alerts you when your match result is officially recorded in the tournament bracket.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Community

Control notifications from other players and community activity.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="New followers">
    Sent when another player follows your profile.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Messages received">
    Notifies you when you receive a direct message. You can set this to deliver immediately or in a daily digest.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Mention in a post">
    Alerts you when someone tags your username in a community post, match comment, or league discussion.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Venues

Notifications tied to saved venues and your check-in history.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check-in confirmed">
    Sent when your check-in at a venue is recorded. Useful for confirming that loyalty points or match logs are being tracked correctly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Venue hours changed">
    Alerts you when a saved venue updates its operating hours, so you never show up to a closed hall.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="New review on a saved venue">
    Notifies you when a new review is posted for one of your saved or favorited venues.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Account

System-level notifications about your account status and activity.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Security alerts">
    Sent for login attempts from new devices, password changes, and linked account modifications. These notifications cannot be fully disabled — see the note below.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Promotional emails">
    Occasional emails about new features, platform events, and partner offers. You can unsubscribe at any time from within the email or from this settings page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Weekly stats digest">
    A summary delivered every Monday covering your match record, handicap movement, and ranking changes from the previous week.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  Security alerts cannot be fully disabled. Even if you turn off all other Account notifications, you will continue to receive email notifications for new device logins, password resets, and changes to your linked accounts. This is to protect your account from unauthorized access.
</Note>

## Email vs. push notification settings

For most notification types, you can choose your delivery channel independently. This means you can receive a push notification for time-sensitive alerts like **Match called** while only getting an email for lower-urgency updates like **Standings updated**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email notifications">
    Email notifications are sent to the address on file for your account. To update your email address, go to **Account → Settings → Account details**. You can set a daily send window to batch non-urgent emails into a single digest rather than receiving them individually.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Push notifications">
    Push notifications are delivered to the Billiards.com mobile app on your registered devices. To receive push notifications, you must grant notification permissions to the app on your device. You can manage registered devices under **Account → Settings → Devices**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="In-app alerts">
    In-app alerts appear in the notification bell in the top navigation bar whenever you are logged in. These cannot be disabled independently — they mirror whichever notification types you have enabled. You can mark all alerts as read from the notification panel.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
