---

Most issues on Billiards.com can be resolved quickly with a few targeted steps. Work through the relevant section below, and if the problem persists after following the steps, [contact our support team](/help/contact) with a description of what you tried.

<Warning>
  If you believe your account has been compromised, [contact support immediately](/help/contact) and change your password.
</Warning>

## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't log in">
    If you're unable to sign in to your account, work through the following steps in order:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Double-check your credentials">
        Confirm you're using the correct email address. Passwords are case-sensitive — make sure Caps Lock is off.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reset your password">
        Click **Forgot password?** on the login page, enter your email, and follow the link sent to your inbox. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Clear your browser cache and cookies">
        Cached login data can sometimes interfere with authentication. In your browser settings, clear cookies and cached files for `billiards.com`, then try logging in again.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Try a different browser or device">
        If clearing the cache doesn't help, open an incognito/private window or try a different browser entirely (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). This rules out browser extension conflicts.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check if your account is suspended">
        If you see a message about your account being suspended or restricted, [contact support](/help/contact) for details. Do not create a new account — that may extend the suspension.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
      If you signed up using Google or Apple sign-in, use that same method to log in. Email/password login won't work for accounts created through a social sign-in provider.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Venue map not loading">
    If the venue map shows a blank screen, spins indefinitely, or places you in the wrong location, try these fixes:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check location permissions">
        The map requires location access to show venues near you. On mobile, go to Settings → Billiards.com → Location and set it to **While Using the App**. On a desktop browser, click the lock icon in the address bar and allow location access for this site.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable location services on your device">
        Make sure location services are turned on globally: on iOS, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services; on Android, go to Settings → Location and ensure it's enabled.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Disable your VPN">
        VPNs can report an incorrect location to the browser, causing the map to load venues in the wrong region or fail entirely. Temporarily disable your VPN and reload the page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Refresh the page">
        Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to do a hard refresh, bypassing any cached map tiles.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Search by location manually">
        If you'd rather not share your location, type a city name, zip code, or venue name in the search bar above the map to find venues without GPS.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stats not updating after a match">
    Match statistics and standings don't always update immediately. Here's how to check and resolve the issue:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Wait a few minutes">
        After submitting a match result, allow up to 10 minutes for stats to propagate across your profile, the league standings, and your opponent's profile.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the match was submitted, not saved as a draft">
        Open **My Matches** and look for the match in question. If it shows a **Draft** badge, it was saved but not officially submitted. Open it and tap **Submit Result** to finalize it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Re-submit the match if needed">
        If the match shows as submitted but stats still haven't updated after 30 minutes, open the match detail and tap **Re-submit**. This queues it for reprocessing without creating a duplicate entry.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check that your opponent confirmed the result">
        Some leagues require both players to confirm a match result before it counts. Ask your opponent to open the match and tap **Confirm**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      Handicap rating recalculations run nightly. Your rating number may not reflect the latest match until the following morning.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Not receiving notifications">
    If you're not getting match reminders, league updates, or other alerts, check the following:

    **For push notifications (mobile app):**

    1. Go to your device settings, find the Billiards.com app, and confirm that **Notifications** are enabled.
    2. Make sure **Do Not Disturb** or **Focus** mode isn't silencing the app.
    3. Inside the Billiards.com app, go to **Settings → Notifications** and verify that the notification types you want are switched on.

    **For email notifications:**

    1. Check your spam or junk folder for emails from `noreply@billiards.com`.
    2. Add that address to your contacts or safe senders list.
    3. In your Billiards.com account under **Settings → Email Preferences**, confirm your email address is correct and that the relevant notification categories are enabled.

    **For in-app alerts:**

    1. Tap the bell icon in the app. If the list is empty, there may simply be no recent activity to report.
    2. Pull down to refresh the notifications list.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="League standings look wrong">
    League standings are recalculated automatically after each match result is verified and confirmed. If something looks off:

    1. **Wait for pending matches to process** — standings that appear out of order may be waiting on unconfirmed results from other players in the division.
    2. **Confirm all your recent results are submitted** — a missing match could affect your position. Check **My Matches** for any drafts.
    3. **Review the tiebreaker rules** — if two players have identical win/loss records, your league may use head-to-head results, points differential, or other criteria to separate them. Check your league's rules page for details.
    4. **Contact your league organizer** — if you believe there's a genuine error in the standings (such as a match being credited to the wrong player), reach out to your organizer directly. They can review and correct individual results.

    <Tip>
      League organizers have a dedicated standings management dashboard. Most standing disputes are resolved fastest by going directly to them rather than through general support.
    </Tip>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't register for a tournament">
    If the registration button is unavailable or you receive an error when trying to sign up for a tournament, check the following:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Check eligibility requirements">
        Many tournaments require a minimum number of rated matches (typically 10–20) and a verified handicap rating before you can enter. Your profile page shows your current match count and rating. If you're below the threshold, play more rated matches to qualify.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm registration is open">
        Tournament registration windows have a start and end date. If registration hasn't opened yet, the button will be inactive. If it has closed, you'll see an **Registration Closed** notice on the tournament page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check if entry fees are required">
        Some tournaments require payment to complete registration. If you're seeing a payment screen, follow the prompts to pay the entry fee. Registration is not complete until payment is confirmed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Verify you meet division requirements">
        Tournaments often have divisions by handicap range or age group. If your current rating falls outside the division's range, you'll need to register for a different division or wait until your rating adjusts.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Contact the tournament organizer">
        If you meet all the requirements and still can't register, use the **Contact Organizer** link on the tournament page to reach out directly. They can manually add you or identify the issue.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mobile app crashing">
    If the Billiards.com app closes unexpectedly or becomes unresponsive, try the following steps:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Update the app">
        Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android), search for Billiards.com, and install any available update. Most crashes are fixed in the latest release.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart the app">
        Fully close the app (swipe it away from your recent apps list) rather than just minimizing it, then reopen it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Restart your device">
        A full device restart clears memory and resolves many app stability issues. Power off your phone completely, wait 10 seconds, and turn it back on.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Clear the app cache">
        On Android: go to Settings → Apps → Billiards.com → Storage → Clear Cache. On iOS, cache clearing requires reinstalling the app.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reinstall the app">
        Uninstall Billiards.com, restart your device, then reinstall from the App Store or Google Play. Your account data is stored server-side, so you won't lose any stats or history.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
      If the app crashes consistently on a specific screen or action, note what you were doing when it crashed and include that in your report to support — it helps the engineering team identify the issue faster.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
