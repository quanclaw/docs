The Billiards.com support team is here to help with account issues, billing questions, technical problems, and anything else you can't resolve on your own. Choose the contact method that works best for your situation, and we'll get you back to the table as quickly as possible.

<Tip>
  Check the [FAQ](/help/faq) and [Troubleshooting](/help/troubleshooting) pages first — most common issues have quick solutions there.
</Tip>

## Support options

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Email support" icon="envelope" href="mailto:support@billiards.com">
    Send a message to **support@billiards.com** for general inquiries, account issues, billing questions, and anything that doesn't require an immediate response. We respond within 1–2 business days.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Live chat" icon="message" href="https://www.billiards.com/chat">
    Start a live chat session from the in-app help menu or from the website. Chat is available Monday through Friday, 9am–6pm ET. Most sessions are connected within 5 minutes during business hours.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Community forum" icon="users" href="https://www.billiards.com/community">
    Post a question in the community forum to get help from experienced players and Billiards.com staff. Great for strategy questions, league rule interpretations, and feature suggestions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What to include in your request

Providing clear details upfront helps us resolve your issue faster and avoids back-and-forth. Whether you're using email or live chat, include the following:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Your username">
    Include your Billiards.com username or the email address on your account so we can locate your profile immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A description of the issue">
    Describe what's happening in plain terms. For example: "My handicap rating dropped from 520 to 480 after I won a match on April 18th." The more specific, the better.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Steps to reproduce the problem">
    If the issue is repeatable, tell us exactly what you did before it occurred. For example: "I tapped Submit on the match result screen, saw a confirmation message, but the match never appeared in My Matches."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Screenshots or screen recordings, if applicable">
    Visual evidence is extremely helpful for diagnosing display bugs, error messages, or unexpected behavior. Attach screenshots directly to your email or share them with the live chat agent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Response time expectations

| Channel | Availability | Typical response time |
|---|---|---|
| Email | 24/7 submission | 1–2 business days |
| Live chat | Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm ET | Under 5 minutes |
| Community forum | 24/7 | Varies (community-driven) |

Response times may be longer during peak periods, such as tournament weekends or the start of a new league season. If your issue is urgent — for example, a compromised account or a time-sensitive tournament registration problem — use live chat or mark your email subject line as **Urgent**.

<Note>
  For league disputes, contact your league organizer directly first. Organizers have the tools to review and correct results, adjust standings, and address player conduct issues within their league. Our support team can escalate on your behalf if the organizer is unresponsive.
</Note>
