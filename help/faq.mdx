Whether you're new to Billiards.com or a seasoned league player, the answers below cover the questions our support team hears most often. Browse by topic to find what you're looking for, and reach out if you need more help.

## Account & profile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Billiards.com free to use?">
    Creating an account and using the core features of Billiards.com is completely free. You can search for venues, track your match history, join public leagues, and connect with other players at no cost.

    Some advanced features require a paid subscription or a one-time fee, including:

    - **Advanced stats dashboards** — detailed breakdowns of your performance trends, shot accuracy, and opponent history
    - **Tournament entry** — certain sanctioned tournaments charge an entry fee that is collected through the platform
    - **Premium league tiers** — some league organizers offer premium divisions with enhanced features

    Any fees are clearly shown before you commit to a purchase.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I reset my password?">
    If you can't log in or have forgotten your password, follow these steps:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the login page">
        Click **Log in** at the top of any page, then select **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter your email address">
        Type the email address associated with your Billiards.com account and click **Send reset link**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check your inbox">
        You'll receive an email with a password reset link within a few minutes. The link is valid for 30 minutes.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check your spam folder">
        If the email doesn't arrive, check your spam or junk folder. Add `noreply@billiards.com` to your contacts to prevent future filtering.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Contact support if needed">
        If you still haven't received the email after 10 minutes and it's not in spam, [contact our support team](/help/contact) with your username or the email address on your account.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple accounts?">
    No. Billiards.com permits one account per person. Multiple accounts distort handicap ratings, league standings, and the integrity of the platform for all players.

    If you accidentally created a duplicate account, [contact support](/help/contact) and we'll merge your history into your primary account. Accounts found to be duplicates may be suspended or removed without notice.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Leagues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the handicap system work?">
    Billiards.com uses a BCA-style skill-based handicap system. Your handicap rating is a number that reflects your current playing level relative to other players on the platform.

    The rating adjusts after each recorded match based on two factors:

    - **Your current rating vs. your opponent's rating** — beating a higher-rated player earns you more points than beating a lower-rated player
    - **Win rate** — your rating trends toward the level at which you win roughly half your matches against similarly rated opponents

    New accounts start with a provisional rating that stabilizes after approximately 20 rated matches. Ratings are recalculated nightly, so you may not see a change immediately after a match is reported.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my opponent no-shows?">
    If your opponent fails to appear for a scheduled match, here's what to do:

    1. Wait at least 15 minutes past the scheduled start time before reporting the no-show.
    2. Open the match in the app and tap **Report Issue → Opponent No-Show**.
    3. The league organizer will review the report, typically within 24–48 hours.
    4. If the no-show is confirmed, you'll receive a forfeit win, which counts toward your record and standings.

    Repeated no-shows by any player may result in league suspension. If you need to reschedule a match, always communicate with your opponent and league organizer ahead of time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I leave a league mid-season?">
    Yes, you can withdraw from a league before the season ends. To do so, go to **My Leagues**, select the league, and choose **Leave League** from the settings menu.

    Keep in mind the following before withdrawing:

    - Your results up to that point remain in the standings and count toward other players' records.
    - Depending on the league rules, you may forfeit any remaining scheduled matches, which can affect division standings.
    - Some leagues restrict future eligibility for players who leave mid-season. Check the league rules or ask your organizer.
    - Entry fees are generally non-refundable after a season has started.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Stats & matches

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I correct a match result I entered incorrectly?">
    Yes, match results can be edited within **24 hours** of submission. To correct a result:

    1. Navigate to **My Matches** in your profile.
    2. Find the match and tap **Edit Result**.
    3. Update the score and confirm.

    After 24 hours, the edit window closes to preserve standings integrity. If you need a correction after that point, contact your league organizer directly — they have the ability to adjust results within their league. For matches outside of a league context, [contact support](/help/contact) with the match ID and the correct score.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did my handicap rating go down after I won?">
    Your rating is based on *expected* performance, not just wins and losses. If you were rated significantly higher than your opponent, the system expected you to win — so a win produces only a small rating increase, while a loss produces a larger decrease.

    For example, if you're rated 600 and your opponent is rated 400, the system already expects you to win around 75% of the time. Winning that match confirms expectations rather than exceeding them, so your rating may barely move or could dip slightly depending on the margin.

    Over time, your rating settles at the level that accurately reflects your skill. This is normal and works the same for every player on the platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do guest matches count toward my stats?">
    Yes. When you record a match against a guest (someone without a Billiards.com account), the result appears in your match history and counts toward your win/loss record.

    However, because the guest doesn't have a verified rating, the match is counted as an **unrated** result. It will appear in your profile stats but will not affect your handicap rating in either direction. Your guest's result is not saved to any account since they are not registered on the platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Venues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I claim my venue listing?">
    If you own or manage a billiards venue that already appears on Billiards.com, you can request to claim and verify the listing so you can keep information accurate and respond to player reviews.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find your venue">
        Search for your venue by name or address on the Venues page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the venue profile">
        Click on the venue to open its full listing page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click 'Claim this venue'">
        Scroll to the bottom of the listing and click the **Claim this venue** link.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Submit verification details">
        You'll be prompted to provide your name, business role, and a contact email. Our team will verify ownership and get back to you within 3–5 business days.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    Once verified, you can update hours, contact info, table counts, photos, and amenities directly from your dashboard.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is my venue's information wrong?">
    Venue listings are initially created from public data and may be outdated. If you spot incorrect information — such as wrong hours, a closed venue, or an incorrect address — you can flag it without claiming the listing.

    On the venue's profile page, scroll down and click **Report an Issue**. Select the type of correction (hours, contact info, permanent closure, etc.) and describe what's wrong. Our content team reviews submissions and updates listings typically within 2–3 business days.

    If you are the venue owner and want direct control over your listing, see "How do I claim my venue listing?" above.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which browsers and devices are supported?">
    Billiards.com works on all modern browsers, including:

    - **Chrome** 110 and later
    - **Firefox** 110 and later
    - **Safari** 16 and later
    - **Edge** 110 and later

    Internet Explorer is not supported. If you're using an older browser version and experiencing layout issues, updating your browser should resolve them.

    **Mobile apps** are available for both iOS (iPhone and iPad, iOS 15+) and Android (Android 10+). You can download them from the App Store and Google Play respectively. The mobile apps include all core features plus push notifications for match reminders and league updates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is the app not showing venues near me?">
    The venue map relies on your device's location services to show nearby venues. If the map appears empty or shows venues in the wrong area, check the following:

    1. **Location permissions** — Go to your device's Settings, find the Billiards.com app, and make sure location access is set to **While Using the App** or **Always**.
    2. **Location services enabled** — Confirm that location services are turned on globally in your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Location Services on iOS; Settings → Location on Android).
    3. **Browser permissions** — If you're using the website, your browser may have blocked location access. Click the lock icon in the address bar and set location to **Allow**.
    4. **Manual search** — If you'd prefer not to share your location, you can always search for venues by city, zip code, or venue name using the search bar on the Venues page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
