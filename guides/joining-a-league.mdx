---
title: "How to Join a Billiards League on Billiards.com"
description: "A step-by-step guide to finding and joining a local billiards league on Billiards.com, from browsing options to playing your first match."
---

Joining a league is one of the best ways to improve your game, meet other players, and stay consistent at the table. Billiards.com connects you with local leagues across game formats and skill levels, so whether you're picking up a cue for the first time or competing seriously, there's a league that fits your schedule and ability.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse available leagues">
    Navigate to the **Leagues** section from the top menu. Use the filter panel to narrow results by game type (8-ball, 9-ball, 10-ball, straight pool), skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced), location (city or zip code), and day of the week. Each league card shows the format, meeting location, and how many spots remain.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review league details">
    Click any league to open its full detail page. Here you'll find the match format, weekly schedule, session length, entry fee (if any), house rules, and a list of current members. Many leagues also include a short description from the organizer with notes on atmosphere and expectations. Take a moment to read through everything so you know what you're signing up for before you commit.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Join League'">
    When you're ready, click the **Join League** button on the league detail page.

    - **Open leagues** — your membership is confirmed immediately and you're added to the roster.
    - **Private leagues** — your request is sent to the league organizer for approval. You'll receive a notification once they accept or decline, typically within 24–48 hours.

    <Note>
      If a league is full, you can join the waitlist and be notified automatically when a spot opens up.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your schedule and first match date">
    After joining, your league schedule appears in the **My Leagues** section of your dashboard. Review the match calendar, note your first scheduled match date, and confirm the venue address and start time. If the league uses a rotating schedule or handicap pairings, your first opponent is typically assigned a few days before the match.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in before your first match to confirm attendance">
    Before your first match, open the Billiards.com app or website and confirm your attendance on the match details screen. This lets your opponent and league organizer know you're showing up and prevents forfeits from scheduling confusion. If something comes up and you can't make it, use the same screen to notify the organizer as early as possible.
  </Step>

  <Step title="After your match, submit the result in the app">
    Once your match is finished, open the app and navigate to your scheduled match in **My Leagues**. Enter the final score for each game, then submit the result. Both players receive a confirmation, and the league standings update automatically. Your handicap rating adjusts based on the outcome, so results carry weight — submit them the same night while the details are fresh.

    <Tip>
      If you're new to competitive play, look for leagues that use a handicap system. Handicap leagues level the playing field by adjusting your required wins based on your skill rating, so beginners can compete meaningfully against more experienced players from day one.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
