---
title: "Find the Best Pool Halls and Billiards Venues"
description: "How to use Billiards.com's venue finder to discover, review, and save nearby pool halls and billiards rooms, whether you're at home or traveling."
---

Finding a good place to play shouldn't take longer than the drive there. The Billiards.com venue finder pulls together pool halls, billiards rooms, and bars with tables near you — complete with hours, photos, table types, and reviews from the community. Whether you're looking for a serious room with nine-footers or a casual spot for a few bar games, you can find and evaluate venues before you ever leave the house.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Venues in the top navigation or tap the map icon in the mobile app">
    On desktop, select **Venues** from the main navigation bar. On mobile, tap the map pin icon at the bottom of the screen. Both views open the venue finder with your current region loaded by default.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Allow location access or enter your city or zip code">
    For the most accurate results, allow Billiards.com to access your location when prompted. If you prefer not to share your location, or if you're searching for venues in a different area, type a city name or zip code into the search bar at the top of the map. The finder updates results to center on that area.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse the map or list view">
    Toggle between **Map** and **List** view using the buttons in the top-right corner of the venue finder.

    - **Map view** shows venue pins across the area. Tap a pin to see a summary card with the venue name, distance from your search point, current hours, and star rating.
    - **List view** sorts venues by distance and displays the same summary details in a scrollable format, which is easier to scan when you have several options close together.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply filters to narrow your results">
    Use the **Filters** panel to focus on venues that match what you're looking for:

    - **Open now** — shows only venues currently within their posted hours
    - **Table type** — filter by bar tables, regulation 9-foot tables, or snooker tables
    - **Amenities** — narrow by features like league hosting, food service, private room rentals, or coin-operated tables

    Active filters appear as tags below the search bar. Remove any filter by clicking the × on its tag.

    <Tip>
      Check-ins count toward your community activity score and let friends know where you're playing. If you visit a venue regularly, checking in also helps surface it in recommendations for players with similar activity in your area.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap a venue to see its full detail page">
    Click or tap any venue to open its full listing. The detail page includes:

    - **Photos** — submitted by the venue or community members
    - **Hours** — posted weekly schedule, including holiday exceptions when available
    - **Table count and types** — number of tables and what size or format they are
    - **Fees** — hourly rates, per-game pricing, or membership info if applicable
    - **Reviews** — written feedback and star ratings from Billiards.com members who have checked in or played there

    If a detail seems out of date, use the **Suggest an Edit** link at the bottom of the listing to flag it for review.

    <Note>
      If you own or manage a billiards venue, you can claim your listing to keep hours, photos, and details current. Visit the **Venue Owners** section under your account settings to start the verification process. Claimed listings are marked with a verified badge and rank higher in search results.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save to Favorites or tap 'Check In' when you arrive">
    From any venue detail page, tap the **bookmark icon** to save it to your Favorites. Saved venues appear in the **My Venues** section of your profile for quick access later — useful when you travel to a new city and want to revisit good spots you found.

    When you physically arrive at a venue, tap **Check In** to log your visit. Check-ins are visible to your followers, contribute to your community activity, and count toward venue-based badges and challenges.
  </Step>
</Steps>
