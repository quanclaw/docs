Your Billiards.com account is more than a login — it's your player identity on the platform. A complete profile helps other players find and recognize you, enables accurate league matching, and gives you a meaningful stats record from day one. This guide covers everything from creating your account to fine-tuning your preferences.

## Creating your account

You can create a Billiards.com account using an email address or by signing in with an existing social account.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email signup">
    1. Go to [billiards.com](https://www.billiards.com) and click **Sign up**.
    2. Enter your email address and choose a strong password (minimum 8 characters).
    3. Click **Create account**.
    4. Open the confirmation email from Billiards.com and click **Verify email address**.
    5. You are redirected to the profile setup screen to complete your account.

    <Warning>
      The verification link in your confirmation email expires after 24 hours. If it expires before you click it, return to the login page and request a new verification email from the sign-in screen.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Social login">
    1. Go to [billiards.com](https://www.billiards.com) and click **Sign up**.
    2. Click **Continue with Google** or **Continue with Facebook**.
    3. Authorize Billiards.com to access your basic account information.
    4. You are taken directly to the profile setup screen — no email verification required.

    Your social account email is used for notifications. You can change your notification preferences at any time from **Settings > Notifications**.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Setting up your profile

After creating your account, the profile setup screen walks you through the core fields that personalize your experience on the platform.

### Display name

Your display name appears on leaderboards, match records, league standings, and your public profile. Choose something that identifies you to other players in your local community — many players use their real name or a well-known nickname.

You can change your display name once every 30 days from **Settings > Profile**.

### Location

Enter your city or ZIP code to enable nearby venue search and local league recommendations. Your precise address is never shown publicly — only your city and state appear on your profile.

### Skill level

Select the skill level that best describes your current game:

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| **Beginner** | Learning the fundamentals, inconsistent position play, fewer than 1 year playing regularly |
| **Intermediate** | Comfortable running short racks, developing safety play and position routes |
| **Advanced** | Consistent ball-pocketing, strong positional game, competitive in local leagues |
| **Pro** | Tournament-level play, advanced pattern play, consistent under pressure |

<Note>
  Your skill level is used to calculate your handicap rating and match you with appropriate leagues. It does not need to be perfect at signup — your handicap adjusts automatically as you log matches, so it converges to an accurate value over time.
</Note>

### Preferred games

Select all the game formats you play or want to play. This helps the platform recommend relevant leagues and surface tournaments in your preferred disciplines.

- **8-ball** — the most widely played format in recreational and league play
- **9-ball** — rotation game, popular in competitive and televised play
- **10-ball** — similar to 9-ball with called shots, considered a more skill-intensive format
- **Straight pool** (14.1 continuous) — point-based format requiring consistent run-building
- **One-pocket** — strategic format where each player targets a single corner pocket

You can update your game preferences at any time from **Settings > Profile**.

## Profile photo and bio

A profile photo and bio make your account easier to recognize and help build your presence in the community.

**To add a profile photo:**

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Click the avatar placeholder at the top of the page.
3. Upload a photo from your device. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP. Maximum size: 5 MB.
4. Crop and position the image using the editor, then click **Save photo**.

**To add a bio:**

The bio field supports up to 280 characters. Use it to share your home venue, favorite game format, how long you've been playing, or anything else you want other players to know. Your bio appears on your public profile page and in league rosters.

## Linking social accounts

Connecting social accounts to your Billiards.com profile makes it easier for friends to find you and lets you share match results or achievements directly to your social feeds.

To link a social account:

1. Go to **Settings > Profile**.
2. Scroll to the **Linked accounts** section.
3. Click **Connect** next to the platform you want to link (Google, Facebook, or Instagram).
4. Authorize the connection in the popup window.

You can disconnect a linked account at any time from the same section. Disconnecting a social account does not delete your Billiards.com account or any of your data.

<Info>
  If you created your Billiards.com account using Google or Facebook, that social account is already linked. You can add additional social connections from the **Linked accounts** section.
</Info>
